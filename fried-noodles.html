Fried Noodles

Fried Noodles are a savory, satisfying dish made with perfectly crispy noodles stir-fried with a medley of fresh vegetables and a flavorful blend of seasonings. The noodles are golden and crispy on the outside, yet tender on the inside, creating a perfect balance of textures. Whether enjoyed as a main dish or a side, this dish is quick, delicious, and bursting with flavor in every bite. This specific recipe aims at making it more acceisble to everyone regardless of your social stature.

  • 15-20mins
  • Serves 1

Ingredients

  • Indomie or any noodles of your choice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Paprika
  • Green onions (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook the noodles: Boil the noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. 2. Prepare the veggies: Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the sliced onion, bell pepper, and carrot. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften but still have a little crunch.
  3. 3. Add garlic: Add the minced garlic to the pan and stir-fry for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. 4. Fry the noodles: Add the cooked noodles to the pan with the veggies. Stir-fry everything together for about 2-3 minutes, letting the noodles get a little crispy at the edges.
  5. 5. Season: Pour in the soy sauce. Toss everything to coat the noodles evenly. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. 6. Garnish & Serve: Once the noodles are well-coated and slightly crispy, transfer them to a plate. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired, and serve hot!
