Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich, creamy dish featuring tender chicken simmered in a velvety tomato-based sauce infused with a blend of aromatic spices. Its smooth texture and balanced flavors make it a comforting and indulgent classic that’s perfect when served with naan or rice.

  • 35mins
  • Serves 5

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup cooking cream
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder (optional for heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. 1. Cook the chicken: Heat a tablespoon of butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned on all sides (about 5-7 minutes). Remove the chicken and set it aside.
  2. 2. Prepare the sauce: In the same pan, melt the remaining tablespoon of butter. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté for 3-4 minutes, until softened and fragrant.
  3. 3. Spices & tomatoes: Add the garam masala, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder (if using). Stir for about 1 minute, then pour in the crushed tomatoes. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken.
  4. 4. Finish the sauce: Once the sauce has thickened, add the heavy cream and stir to combine. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and simmer for another 5-7 minutes, allowing the chicken to absorb the flavors and heat through.
  5. 5.Garnish & serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with naan bread or rice.
