Stir Fry Beef

Stir-Fried Beef is a savory, tender dish featuring thinly sliced beef cooked with vibrant vegetables and tossed in a rich, smoky BBQ sauce. The combination of sweet and tangy flavors with a hint of spice creates a mouthwatering, satisfying meal. Perfect for a quick dinner, it pairs wonderfully with rice or noodles for a complete, flavorful feast.

  • 20mins
  • Serves 5

Ingredients

  • Indomie or any noodles of your choice
  • 300g beef (flank steak or sirloin), thinly sliced against the grain
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoons BBQ sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. 1. Prepare the beef: Thinly slice the beef against the grain. Set aside.
  2. 2. Stir-fry the veggies: Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add the sliced onion, bell pepper, and zucchini. Stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until the veggies are tender but still crisp. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. 3. Cook the beef: In the same pan, add the remaining tablespoon of oil. Add the sliced beef in a single layer. Let it sear without stirring for about 1-2 minutes to get a good brown color. Stir-fry the beef for another 3-4 minutes until cooked through.
  4. 4. Add garlic and ginger: Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the beef, stir-frying for another minute until fragrant.
  5. 5. Make the sauce: In a small bowl, mix together BBQ sauce, honey, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Pour this sauce over the beef and stir to coat the meat evenly. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. 6. Combine and serve: Return the cooked veggies to the pan and toss everything together. Stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes to heat through. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  7. 6. Garnish and serve: Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds, if desired. Serve with steamed rice or noodles.
