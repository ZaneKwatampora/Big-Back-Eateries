Protein Wraps

Protein wraps are a delicious and nutritious meal, filled with lean proteins like chicken, turkey, or tofu, fresh veggies, and a flavorful spread, all wrapped in a soft tortilla. Packed with protein and fiber, these wraps are a perfect, quick, and satisfying option for lunch or a healthy on-the-go snack. Customize them with your favorite ingredients for a tasty, balanced meal that fits any craving.

  • 10mins
  • Serves 1

Ingredients

  • 1 large whole wheat or spinach tortilla
  • 100g grilled chicken breast (or any protein of choice: turkey, beef, tofu)
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: hot sauce or your favorite dressing

Instructions

  1. 1. Pre trepahe protein: If not already done, cook your protein of choice (e.g., grill the chicken breast, sauté tofu, or use cooked leftovers). Slice into thin strips or bite-sized pieces.
  2. 2. Assemble the wrap: Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  3. 3. Add the veggies and protein: Layer the lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and avocado on top of the spread. Add your sliced protein on top.
  4. 4. Season and fold: Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. If you like extra flavor, drizzle with hot sauce or dressing of choice. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward, then roll it up tightly from the bottom.
  5. 5. Serve: Slice the wrap in half and serve immediately!
